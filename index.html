About

Scott Machine Tool has been providing custom tooling, production fixtures, precision gage components, and prototype parts to a variety of area industries since 2002.

In-house maching includes: CNC Milling, Turning, Grinding (surface and cylindrical) as well as full design capabilities utilizing either AutoCad or SolidWorks.

Scott Machine is owned and operated by Journeyman Tool and Die Maker, Perry Scott with 30 plus years experience in the tooling industry.
